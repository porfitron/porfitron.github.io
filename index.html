Porfirio José Landeros

Product Marketing Executive

Porfirio is a seasoned Product Marketing Executive with a track record of driving growth and innovation in social media and digital content spaces. He is passionate about bridging the gap between product development and market success, leveraging data-driven strategies to connect with audiences.

When not immersed in product strategy, Porfirio volunteers as a Disaster Service Worker with the NERT/SFFD. He is a retired, nationally ranked ballroom dance champion, who now enjoys long walks with his wife and two dogs, and gravel cycling Bay Area trails.

He is also available for Advisory and Consulting roles in areas he holds dear, including health & wellness, streaming media, and content delivery platforms (including AR/VR and wearables).
