Betsie – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

Betsie is a social app for friendly, non-monetary predictions shared among adults. Betsie does not support gambling, real-money wagers, or prizes of monetary value.

This Privacy Policy explains the information we collect, how we use it—including for advertising—and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

A. Account & Profile Information

  • Name or username
  • Email
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Age confirmation (18+)

B. App Usage Data

  • Predictions and “bets” you create
  • Outcomes you record
  • Comments, reactions, social interactions
  • Activity logs and features you use

C. Device & Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Device type and OS
  • App version
  • Diagnostic and crash data

D. Advertising & Analytics Data

To support our business, we may collect data for:

  • App measurement
  • Ad performance
  • Basic interest categories
  • General location (approximate, not precise GPS)

We do not collect sensitive categories (health, race, sexual orientation, political beliefs).

E. Optional Integrations

If you link social accounts or contacts, we only collect what’s required to help you find friends.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and improve the app
  • Personalize your experience
  • Display predictions and social interactions
  • Provide safety and moderation
  • Show ads, including personalized ads where permitted
  • Measure ad and feature performance

You may opt out of personalized advertising where applicable under local regulations. We do not sell personal information in the traditional “for money” sense.

3. Advertising

We may show:

  • Non-personalized ads
  • Contextual ads
  • Personalized ads (based on your interactions, device info, and ad partner signals)

We partner with advertising service providers who may receive device-level identifiers.

We do not allow advertisers access to:

  • Bet content
  • Personal messages
  • Sensitive user attributes

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Content moderation tools
  • Legal authorities when necessary

We do not share bets, predictions, or sensitive user content with advertisers.

5. Age Requirements

Betsie is for adults 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account remains active.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access or correct your data
  • Request deletion
  • Opt out of personalized ads where legally required
  • Request details on our data processing

8. Contact Us

Privacy questions: support@betsie.app
